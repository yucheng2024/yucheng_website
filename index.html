Yucheng Xie

Yucheng Xie is a tenure-track assistant professor in the Graduate Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Yeshiva University. His research focuses on smart healthcare and mobile computing, wireless sensing and authentication, and AI security, with an emphasis on robust intelligent systems for real-world deployment.
